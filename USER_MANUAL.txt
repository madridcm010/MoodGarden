USER MANUAL - MOODGARDEN
1. OVERVIEW
1.
MoodGarden is an emotional well-being application that helps users track their moods, reflect
on their emotional patterns, and receive personalized recommendations. The app includes
mood logging, journaling, a dashboard with insights, and mood-based guidance.
1. GETTING STARTED
1.
2.1 Creating an Account
▪ Open the Sign Up page.
▪ Enter your email, username, and password.
▪ Submit the form to create your account.
2.2 Logging In
▪ Navigate to the Login page.
▪ Enter your credentials.
▪ Upon success, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.
1. MAIN FEATURES
1.
3.1 Dashboard The Dashboard displays:
▪ Your most recent mood
▪ Recent mood logs
▪ AI-generated recommendations
▪ Navigation to Mood Input, Mood Details, and Settings
3.2 Logging Your Mood
▪ Select "Log Mood" from the Dashboard.
▪ Choose your mood category.
▪ Optionally add a journal entry.
▪ Submit to save the mood.
3.3 Viewing Mood Details The Mood Details page shows:
▪ Full mood history

1.

▪ Verify credentials.
▪ Ensure backend server is running.

▪ Check backend API connection.

▪ Confirm API configuration.
